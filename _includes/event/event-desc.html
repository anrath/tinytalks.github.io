A Case for Hope:

Reframing Our Stories, Reclaiming Our Well-Being

How do we live well in a time of uncertainty? How can we get comfortable with discomfort? And how will we share our stories? Discuss these questions and more with us at the latest Tiny Talks event.

Over the last couple years, our members have expressed grappling with their relationship with uncertainty, at times loneliness, and the joys of connection. At the same time, we’ve been exploring different, nuanced perspectives on mental wellness. Through this event, we hope to delve deeper into the relationships all of us have with our emotions.

Join us virtually on Saturday, January 15 at 1:30 PM PST to explore this question and much more.
