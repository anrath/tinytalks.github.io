Exploring the COVID Time Capsule: May 22

What would the future look like if we could shift our gaze from stages and screens to each other? When we break free from the isolation of virtual spaces and have conversations together, we can rediscover what it’s like to be a part of something bigger than ourselves.

The past year has been a time of Both/And. Both loneliness and togetherness. Both instability and constancy. Both confusion and clarity. Both loss and gain. History books will capture the broad brushstrokes of the COVID-19 Pandemic, but not the humanity of your deeply personal experience. What should future generations know?
